About

Classic match-3, tuned for adventure.

At its core, Skull Treasures Match 3 Puzzle is simple: match pieces, clear objectives, and move forward. I’m a sucker for the whole treasure hunting vibe — something about those skull symbols just works for me. The graphics are bright and colorful, and when you pull off a big combo, it really pops on screen. It’s built to be approachable, but it rewards players who plan their moves and learn how to set up bigger chains.
