Features

Everything you want in a match-3 — plus a little extra.

  • Match-3 with unique twists

    The basic gameplay’s still there, but they throw in new stuff that makes you think differently about each level. Some stages ask for careful clearing, others reward aggressive combo-building. You won’t get bored doing the same thing over and over.

  • Rewards that feel earned

    When you beat a level, it actually feels like you accomplished something instead of just going through the motions. Rewards support your next attempt, helping you build confidence as challenges rise. The game keeps you moving without making every win feel identical.

  • A world built around treasure

    The theme isn’t just decoration — it shapes the mood. It's got this cool adventure feel where each level is like uncovering another piece of the puzzle. It’s light adventure energy with a puzzle core.
