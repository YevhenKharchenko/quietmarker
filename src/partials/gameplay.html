Levels

Over 1000 levels, and they don’t all play the same.

Skull Treasures Match 3 Puzzle offers 1000+ levels designed to keep pushing your strategy. The first levels are pretty easy to get you used to things, but later on they really make you think about your moves. The best part is that the challenge doesn’t come only from difficulty — it comes from variety. You’ll keep encountering new tasks that force you to rethink your usual approach.

  • Clear specific tiles and blockers

  • Reach targets with limited moves

  • Build combos to trigger chain reactions

  • Use boosters tactically instead of instantly
